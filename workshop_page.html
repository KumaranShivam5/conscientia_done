Neural
Network

Have you ever noticed that the human mind is so much better than computers at anticipating outcomes just by recognising pattern? How can humans predict the projectile without solving any equations but machines cannot. Neural network is a way to bridge that gap by mimicing the overwhelming complexity and working of the brain, designed to recognize patterns and solve problems. This is widely used in computation to increase performance and also to find patterns where even humans can't. This workshop brought to you by the experts in computer sciences and deep learning will teach about the amazing concept of Neural Network and how to implement it.
